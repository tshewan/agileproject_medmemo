A modern way to track your health

A free service that manages your health information by safely storing your medical report and keeping track of your medication regimen. By scanning your medical QR code, Medmemo offers a simpler way for your doctor to access your health information, produce a new report medicine schedule.

Explore More

Medical Report

Every health concern you have ever had or will ever have can be stored in the Medical Report.

Medicine Schedule

Viewing the doctor-generated medication schedule allows you to keep track of which medications to take when, for which diseases, and at what times.

Generate QR Code

To make life easier for both the patient and the doctor, a user can build or generate their own customized medical QR code. The health information of his patient is immediately accessible to the doctor after scanning the QR code.
